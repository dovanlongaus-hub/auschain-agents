## SaaS Benchmarks 2026 (Australia context)

### Pricing benchmarks
- **License utilization** improved: 47% (2024) → 54% (2025) — users expect more value per seat
- High-profile vendors raising prices → buyers are more price-sensitive, shop around more
- Australian-only buyer pools typically value SaaS 20-30% lower multiples vs global
- Recommended: show clear ROI in pricing page; tie pricing to outcomes, not features

### Churn benchmarks by vertical
| Segment | Monthly Churn |
|---------|--------------|
| Enterprise software | ~0.25% |
| SMB SaaS | ~2-3% |
| EdTech | up to 9.6% |
| Migration/Legal SaaS (est.) | ~1-2% target |

- **Good churn for Auschain**: < 2% monthly for SMB tier, < 0.5% for enterprise
- High NRR (Net Revenue Retention) + low CAC payback → doubles growth rate (High Alpha 2026 data)

### Pricing model recommendations for Auschain
- **Usage-based or outcome-based** pricing trending up in 2026 (tie to visa applications submitted)
- **Freemium → paid** conversion: industry avg 2-5%; aim for 5%+ with strong onboarding
- **Annual billing discount**: 15-20% is the sweet spot; 25%+ can hurt monthly cashflow
- Include **AUD pricing** prominently for AU customers (reduces friction)

### CAC & payback
- **Target CAC payback**: < 12 months for SMB, < 18 months for enterprise
- Pair high NRR with low CAC payback to nearly double Rule of 40 score

## SEO & Content Marketing 2026

### Google algorithm updates — what matters now
- **February 2026 Broad Core Update** (rolled out Feb 1, 2026) — re-ranks based on improved relevance understanding
- **February 2026 Discover Core Update** (Feb 5, 2026) — first-ever Discover-specific update; affects content publishers
- 2026 direction: "less about punishment, more about re-ranking" → quality content wins, don't over-optimize

### What changed from 2025
- Helpful Content System now deeply integrated into core algo (not a separate signal)
- Discover traffic becoming a real channel — optimize for it (images, engaging titles, freshness)
- E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) still paramount

### Practical SEO checklist for Auschain
- ✅ Each visa/migration page should demonstrate **real expertise** (author credentials, process details)
- ✅ Keep content **fresh** — update blog posts quarterly at minimum (Discover rewards recency)
- ✅ Core Web Vitals — LCP < 2.5s, INP < 200ms (replacing FID), CLS < 0.1
- ✅ Structured data for FAQs, reviews, and service pages
- ✅ Target long-tail: "partner visa Australia 2026 cost", "employer sponsored visa requirements"
- ❌ Avoid AI-generated content spam — Google devalues thin, unverified AI content

### Key metric to track
- Organic click-through rate (CTR) — 2026 updates affect positions 4-10 most; optimise meta descriptions
